# Mo, 06.07.2026, 19:30, Notenbank
## Holstentorplatz 2, Lübeck 23552

<br>

The concert takes place at Notenbank, Holstentorplatz 2, directly beside the Holstentor. The venue is a five-minute walk from Lübeck Hauptbahnhof: cross the square in front of the station, head toward the old city gate, and you are there. It is equally close to the Musikhochschule, a short walk through on the bank of the Trave, towards the Holstentor.
